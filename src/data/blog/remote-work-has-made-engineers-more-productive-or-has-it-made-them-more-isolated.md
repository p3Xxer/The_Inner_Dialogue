I work at Amazon. Till 2025, Amazon had two day work from home policy. However, starting 2026 they have mandated five days work from office, but with flexibility of taking one or two days WFH once in two weeks.

Personally I have love working from office, because of two reasons:

1. Your surrounding dictates how you act
2. Ability to socialise and learn from your peers

The drawback of WFO in my opinion is that you don't get uninterrupted focus time and tiredness one feel due to office travel, especially in a city like Bangalore.
